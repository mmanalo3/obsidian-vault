ES74, also known as DS2489 is a bridge device that with a single contact on each side enables up:
- 512kbps pass-through communication
- power delivery for battery charging
- small message exchange
- state reporting between two microcontroller-based system

DS2489 operates from two independent 1-Wire peripheral interfaces, IOA and IOB.
